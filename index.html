MISLA, or Made In South LA, is a south los angeles coding bootcamp, originally started in 2014 in South Central Los Angeles by a team of tech & business savvy South LA residents.

MARTY Members will experience comprehensive training in web design, software programming and other forms of digital literacy, including coding and graphic design.

MARTY Members will be offered hands-on programs that will introduce them to the inner workings of tech & design and entertainment; in the fields of music, marketing, film-making and fashion design.
